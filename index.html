About Better to Bridge

We are a nonprofit organization dedicated to improving medical literacy in all communities by making health information clear, accessible, and practical. We provide educational workshops and resources that help individuals understand their bodies, communicate confidently with healthcare providers, identify misinformation, practice good hygiene, and respond effectively in basic emergency situations. By breaking down complex medical topics into understandable guidance, we work to empower people of all ages and backgrounds to make informed health decisions and take an active role in their wellbeing.

Our Mission

We are a student-led initiative dedicated to bridging the health literacy gap in diverse communities through education, accessibility, and collaboration. Our mission is to empower individuals with the confidence and knowledge to advocate for themselves and make informed health decisions.
